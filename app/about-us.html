About Spatio Tech

Spatio Tech Private Limited is a social enterprise that uses Geographic Information Systems for community development. Through data collection and visualization, we support communities to identify available resources and apply this knowledge in development initiatives. We work with myriad actors, including local residents, governments, non-governmental organizations, small businesses and research institutions. Our collective expertise lies in community organizing, participatory digital mapping, web-based mapping technologies, and data collection and communication. We implement projects that build the capacity of local institutions, and advise organizations on the effective uses of digital maps.
